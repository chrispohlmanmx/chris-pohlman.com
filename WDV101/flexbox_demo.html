Pico De Gallo

Directions

Start by washing and dicing the tomatoes, place the diced tomatoes in a fine mesh strainer and sprinkle lighly with salt. Allow to drain while you continue prepping the remaining ingredients.

Combine all the ingredients together in a bowl and mix. Taste for seasoning and make adjustments accordingly.

Pico De Gallo is best served immediately, but will keep for a day or two in an airtight container in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 4 Roma Tomatoes, Diced
  • 1/2 Medium White Onion, Diced
  • 1 Jalapeno, stemmed, deseeded and minced
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1/2 Bunch of Cilantro, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

Notes

If you'd like a milder Pico then simply add less jalapeno, or use a milder pepper such as a green bell pepper instead.
